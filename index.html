About Nisreen

A psychologist who
started by healing herself

I didn't come to psychology from a textbook. I came from my own story — the moments where I had to sit with difficult feelings and find my way through them. That personal path is what made me want to become the person I once needed: someone who truly shows up.

My training has given me the clinical foundation. But what I bring to every session goes beyond that. I bring real presence. I listen in a way that makes you feel felt, not analysed. I ask questions that open things up rather than shut them down. And I never rush you toward an answer you're not ready for.

I believe therapy is not about fixing what's wrong with you. There is nothing wrong with you. It's about understanding yourself deeply enough to stop being at war with who you are — and finding your way back to peace.

Clinical Approach

Cognitive Behavioural Therapy (CBT)

CBT helps you understand the connection between your thoughts, feelings, and behaviour. Together we identify the thinking patterns that are keeping you stuck — and replace them with ones that actually serve you. Structured, practical, and rooted in evidence.

Evidence-based approach Practical tools you use between sessions Effective for anxiety, depression & overthinking
Clinical Approach

Logo Therapy

Developed by Viktor Frankl, Logo Therapy centres on one question: what gives your life meaning? When nothing makes sense and pain feels pointless, this approach helps you find purpose even inside the difficulty. It's not about eliminating suffering — it's about choosing who you become through it.

Meaning-centred psychotherapy Especially powerful in grief & existential pain Available as single or package sessions
